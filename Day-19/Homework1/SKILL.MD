# Customer Support Skill

Issue Types:
- Refund
- Complaint
- Escalation
- Product Question

Priority Rules:
- Refund => High
- Complaint => Medium
- Escalation => High
- Product Question => Low

Response Templates:

Refund:
We have registered your refund request. Our team will process it within 3 business days.

Complaint:
We apologize for the inconvenience. Your complaint has been logged and will be reviewed.

Escalation:
Your request has been escalated to a senior support specialist.

Product Question:
Thank you for contacting us. We will provide product information shortly.
