Q12. Mini Case Study (5 Marks)
A logistics company processes 10,000 customer support emails daily. They want to: 
(a) automatically classify each email into a category, 
(b) retrieve relevant policy documents to generate a draft reply,
(c) escalate complex cases to a human agent via a ticketing API.

Which framework(s) would you recommend and why? Outline a high-level architecture in 4—6 bullet
points.

# Q12. Mini Case Study – Logistics Email Automation

## Framework Chosen:

LangChain + LlamaIndex + FastAPI

### Step 1:
Receive the customer support email and use LangChain with an LLM to automatically classify it into categories such as delivery issue, refund, payment, or damaged item.

### Step 2:
Use LlamaIndex to search the company's policy documents and retrieve the most relevant information related to the customer's query.

### Step 3:
Use LangChain to combine the customer's email and the retrieved policy information to generate a draft reply.

### Step 4:
If the issue is complex or requires human attention, use FastAPI to call the ticketing API and create a support ticket for a human agent.

## Justification:

LangChain is good for building AI workflows, such as email classification and response generation.
LlamaIndex is specialized in retrieving information from company documents and knowledge bases.
FastAPI makes it easy to integrate with external ticketing systems through APIs.
Together, they automate customer support, improve reply accuracy, and reduce manual effort while handling large volumes of emails efficiently.
